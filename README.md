# ZOOMIES! ⚡ — Luna vs Dolly

A mobile-first, player-vs-computer dog racing game in a single HTML file, set at golden hour.
**Luna** the lemon beagle takes on **Dolly** the Weimaraner over a 250m park sprint — pick your
pup on the title screen and the other one races you.

*Two good girls. One finish line.*

Open [index.html](index.html) in a browser (best on a phone in portrait). Drum the 🐾 pad fast
to run — every tap counts. ⚡ fires ZOOMIES when the meter is full; 🦴 bones restore stamina.
Three difficulties; best times are saved on your device.

Single file, no assets, no build step — canvas + WebAudio, with per-breed body shapes
(a compact lemon-and-white beagle vs a long-legged taupe Weimaraner with the trademark pale eyes).
